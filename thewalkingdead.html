The Walking Dead

Genre: Drama, Horror, Thriller

Release Date: October 31, 2010

Creator: Frank Darabont

Number of Seasons: 11

Synopsis: "The Walking Dead" follows a group of survivors, led by sheriff's deputy Rick Grimes, as they navigate a post-apocalyptic world dominated by zombies, or "walkers". The group struggles to survive and adapt to the new world order while facing threats from both the undead and other surviving humans.

Cast

  • Andrew Lincoln as Rick Grimes
  • Norman Reedus as Daryl Dixon
  • Steven Yeun as Glenn Rhee
  • Chandler Riggs as Carl Grimes
